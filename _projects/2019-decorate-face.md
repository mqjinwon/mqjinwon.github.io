## Overview

A facial decoration application inspired by the SNOW app, enabling real-time overlay of images and animated GIFs onto video feeds.

## Method

- **Framework**: OpenCV + MFC (Microsoft Foundation Classes)
- Real-time face detection and tracking
- GIF animation integration and image overlay
