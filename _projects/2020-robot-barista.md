## Overview

A robotic system that automates drip coffee preparation using a 4-DoF manipulator and computer vision.

## Method

- **Hardware**: Cortex-M4 embedded board for stepper motor control
- **Perception**: YOLO-v3 for target position detection and recognition
- **Task**: Automated water supply for drip coffee brewing
